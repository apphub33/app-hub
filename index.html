Rapid Now — Details

Everything you need to know before installing.

Overview

  • Emergency first: Set one or more emergency contacts and trigger an SOS fast.
  • One‑tap actions: Place a call, send an SMS, or open WhatsApp to your contact with a pre‑filled message.
  • Live GPS (opt‑in): When permitted, the app attaches your current coordinates to your SOS message (e.g., Google Maps link).
  • Privacy: No ads, analytics, or third‑party trackers. Your data stays on your device.
  • Works offline: Calls/SMS work without internet; WhatsApp requires internet.

Key features

  • Save emergency contacts (manual number entry or pick from contacts).
  • Customizable SOS message templates.
  • Optional live GPS link in messages when location is allowed.
  • Big, high‑contrast SOS button for quick access.

Permissions

  • Location (precise GPS) — optional, attaches your coordinates to SOS messages. Not collected by us; no background tracking.
  • Phone / Call — optional, to place an emergency call directly. If denied, the app opens the dialer with the number pre‑filled.
  • SMS — optional, to send SOS texts automatically. If denied, the app opens your SMS app with a pre‑filled message for you to send.
  • Contacts — optional, to pick a contact from your address book. You can also enter numbers manually.
  • Notifications — optional, for confirmations/reminders you enable.

FAQ

Does Rapid Now track me?

No. There are no ads, analytics, or third‑party trackers. Permissions are optional and used only for features you enable.

Why does it ask for location?

Only if you start an SOS that includes GPS. You can deny it; the app still works (without location in the message).

Can I delete my data?

All app data lives on your device. Clear app data in system settings or uninstall to remove it.

Privacy & Data Choices — App Hub

Last updated: August 19, 2025

Your choice. Your control. Our apps do not collect or monitor your personal data. There are no ads, analytics, or third‑party trackers.

Location (GPS) — opt‑in only

  • Some features (like Rapid Now’s SOS) may request your precise (GPS) location. We ask only when a feature needs it and only after you choose to use that feature.
  • Granting location access is optional. You can deny it and the app will still function (without location features).
  • When allowed, location is used only for the requested functionality (e.g., included in your outgoing SOS message). We do not store it on our servers or share it with third parties.
  • No background tracking: we do not continuously track your location.
  • Change permissions anytime in Android Settings → Apps → [App name] → Permissions.

Other permissions

  • Apps may ask for permissions strictly needed for core features (e.g., Phone/Call for direct calling; SMS for sending texts; Contacts for picking numbers; Notifications for alerts).
  • Permissions are requested at runtime and used only for the feature you enable.

What data we collect

  • Website: We do not set cookies. Our host/CDN may keep brief server logs (IP, user‑agent, requested URL) for security and reliability.
  • Apps: By default, apps do not send us personal data. When you trigger an SOS, messages/calls are handled by your device (dialer/SMS/WhatsApp) and sent to your chosen contacts — not to us.
  • Support emails (optional): If you email us, we receive only what you send and use it solely to help you. We’ll delete it on request.

Data storage & deletion

  • App data lives on your device. Delete it anytime by clearing app data or uninstalling.

Third‑party sharing
