Overview

  • Emergency first: Set one or more emergency contacts and trigger an SOS fast.
  • One-tap actions: Call, SMS, or open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.
  • Live GPS (opt-in): When permitted, attaches your coordinates to your SOS message (e.g., Google Maps link).
  • Privacy: No ads, analytics, or third-party trackers. Your data stays on your device.
  • Works offline: Calls/SMS work without internet; WhatsApp needs internet.

Permissions

  • Location (precise GPS) — optional; only to include a location link in your SOS. No background tracking.
  • Phone / Call — optional; place an emergency call directly.
  • SMS — optional; send SOS texts automatically (else we open your SMS app pre-filled).
  • Contacts — optional; pick a contact from your address book (or enter numbers manually).

Privacy & Data Choices — App Hub

Last updated: August 19, 2025

Your choice. Your control. Our apps do not collect or monitor your personal data. There are no ads, analytics, or third-party trackers.

Location (GPS) — opt-in only

  • Some features (like Rapid Now’s SOS) may request your precise (GPS) location only when you use that feature.
  • Granting location access is optional. You can deny it and the app will still function (without location features).
  • We do not store your location on our servers or share it with third parties.
  • No background tracking.

Other permissions

  • Apps may ask for permissions strictly needed for core features (e.g., Phone/Call; SMS; Contacts; Notifications).
  • Permissions are requested at runtime and used only for the feature you enable.

What data we collect

  • Website: We do not set cookies. Our host/CDN may keep brief server logs (IP, user-agent, requested URL) for security and reliability.
  • Apps: By default, apps do not send us personal data. When you trigger an SOS, messages/calls are handled by your device and sent to your chosen contacts — not to us.
  • Support emails (optional): If you email us, we receive only what you send and use it solely to help you. We’ll delete it on request.

Data storage & deletion

  • App data lives on your device. Delete it anytime by clearing app data or uninstalling.

Minors

Our apps are friendly, but not recommended for users under 18. Parents/guardians should supervise any use by minors. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. If an app integrates a third-party service in the future, we’ll disclose it here and in-app before you use that feature.
