▾ Privacy & Data Choices

Last updated: August 19, 2025

Your choice. Your control. Our apps do not collect or monitor your personal data. No ads. No analytics. No third-party trackers.

Location (GPS) — opt-in only

  • Requested only when a feature needs it (e.g., SOS location link).
  • Denying permission keeps the app working (without location features).
  • We don’t store or share your location; no background tracking.

Other permissions

  • Phone/Call, SMS, Contacts, Notifications — only for the features you use.
  • Requested at runtime; your choice every time.

Data

  • Website: No cookies. Our host may keep short server logs for reliability/security.
